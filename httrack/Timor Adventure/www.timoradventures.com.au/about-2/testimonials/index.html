Margot, September 2011, motorcycle tour

The country is geographically diverse with spectacular scenery straight from the “Flinders Ranges “eucalypt strewn rugged gorges to Bali’s lush rain forests and rice paddies, a surprise around ever bend. At no time in Timor have I ever felt unsafe, the children are inquisitive, shy but so keen to learn, both the men and women are helpful and friendly communication is a barrier, however I never felt intimidated or unwelcome.

Timor is our poorest neighbour, yet it is culture rich, I found the poverty there far less an emotional challenge than my trip to India.
