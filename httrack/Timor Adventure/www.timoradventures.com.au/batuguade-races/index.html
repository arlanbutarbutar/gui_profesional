About the races

The Batugude races continues a long tradition of horse racing in Timor Leste that commenced during Portuguese times. Batugude is situated on the Northern coast of Timor-Leste near the border with Indonesia. The name Batugude comes from the Malay term meaning “Big rock” referring to the big rock in the ocean off the coast of Batugude.
