Half and full Day Tours

 

Book online now for a 1/2 day Dili highlights tour

If you want to see all the main sites of Dili then this half day tour is for you. Over the 4 hours of the tour we travel the length of Dili from the top of Cristo Rei through the Resistance Museum  and eventually to historic Tasi Tolu, the site of Pope John Paul’s visit in 1989.

On this tour you don’t just see the sites but learn about them from our experienced guides. We drive, we walk, we talk and we stop for coffee along the way. It is an informative, fun and delicious introduction to Dili

You can book using the form below. This form is not a confirmation of your booking we will be in touch to confirm your booking. Payment is made in cash at the beginning of the tour
